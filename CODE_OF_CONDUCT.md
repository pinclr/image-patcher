# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
image-patcher community a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive,
and healthy community.

## Our Standards

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its Code of
Conduct. Please read the full text for the expected standards of behavior,
enforcement responsibilities, scope, and enforcement guidelines:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: be respectful and constructive, assume good faith, welcome
newcomers, and focus on what is best for the community. Behavior that
harasses, demeans, or excludes others is not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@pinclr.com** (or
**robert@pinclr.com**). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly, and reporters' privacy and security will be respected.

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and
may take appropriate, fair corrective action in response to any behavior they
deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful, up to and including a temporary
or permanent ban from the community.
